# Economy Terms (Student One-Pager)

**Read this before you earn, spend, or sell on the yard.**

BlkSpace uses the **same creator-economy pattern** as Roblox, Fortnite, and other apps: earn soft currency from activity, spend it in the creator shop. Optional BKSPC settlement is a separate on-chain layer.

---

## WeixBucks (WB) — soft currency

- **What:** Platform credits for creating and participating (like Robux or V-Bucks).
- **Earn:** Posts, yards, engagement, node work — see wallet **How to Earn**.
- **Spend:** Tips, boosts, and **creator marketplace** purchases.
- **Cap:** 250 WB per day maximum.
- **Buy with cash?** **No.** Earn-only closed loop (unless a future product is explicitly announced).
- **Cash value promised?** **No.**

## Creator marketplace

- **What:** The yard shop — list your media, mixes, art, themes, services, or tickets.
- **Sell:** Set a price in WB. Buyers pay WB; you receive the **net** amount after the platform fee (5%).
- **Buy:** Browse listings on the wallet **Marketplace** tab and pay with WB you earned.

## Karma

- **What:** Reputation score (like Reddit karma).
- **Spend it?** **No.** It only affects ranking and visibility.
- **Convert to WB?** **No.**

## Tips

- Sending WB includes a **published platform fee** (2%).
- The recipient gets the **net** amount after the fee.

## BKSPC (BlkSpace Settlement)

- **What:** Optional **on-chain** token on Solana when you withdraw earned WB (after eligibility checks). Comparable to Roblox DevEx — settlement of earned credits, not buying WB with cash.
- **Today:** Devnet / simulated — not real mainnet money.
- **Future:** May be **tradable** on exchanges only if legal review approves — not guaranteed today.
- **One-way:** No BKSPC → WB conversion.

## If earn stops or withdraw is denied

- **MIDF throttle:** If abuse signals score above **0.7**, WB earn may pause (see wallet policy panel).
- **Appeal:** Use **Dispute economy decision** on the wallet page. We log your appeal for human review.

## Your responsibility

- WB is for participating on the yard — tips, shop purchases, and optional settlement.
- Scams promising “free BLK” or “guaranteed cashout” are not from BlkSpace.

**Questions:** Yard mod → project lead → counsel-reviewed FAQ (mainnet).
